Use Case: Customer Purchase
--------------------------------------------------
CHARACTERISTIC INFORMATION
Goal in Context: To allow customers to easily purchase customized product
Scope: POS system.
Level: Primary task
Preconditions: User has applicable device and user is near a store
Success End Condition: Satisfied customer and successful payment
Failed End Condition: Customer doesn't order product or cannot pay
Primary Actor: Customer
Trigger: The need to order ice cream product
----------------------------------------
MAIN SUCCESS SCENARIO
1. Customer begins purchase by choosing a physical or digital POS
2. Customer selects dietary restrictions and order customizations
3. Customer waits for and recieves order at a physical location or by delivery (if available)
----------------------
RELATED INFORMATION (optional)
Priority: Top priority.
Performance Target: Customer Satisfied
Frequency: As often as supplies last
Subordinate Use Cases: Backend updates other stakeholders
Channel to primary actor: Backend system, internet, POS device
Secondary Actors: Ice Cream Servers
Channel to Secondary Actors: Backend system, internet, Cashier UI
